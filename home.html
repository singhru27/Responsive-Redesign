Study confirms M&A Advisors Substantially Improve Seller Outcomes

A recent study of 4,468 private business sale transactions found several important results including:

  1. Private sellers receive significantly higher acquisition values when they retain M&A advisers.
  2. A seller has more negotiating leverage when a prospective buyer believes that it is competing with other bidders and M&A advisers help drive acquisition premium by driving competition. The mere presence of an M&A adviser induces a sense of competition.
  3. A sell-side adviser can identify multiple strategic buyers, evaluate the reasonableness of a bidderâ€™s offer and manage multiple bidders.
  4. An M&A adviser can eliminate the M&A information and skill disadvantage that a private seller has against a seasoned acquirer.
